Wildlife

Wildlife refers to the diverse range of plant and animal species that inhabit natural environments, including forests, grasslands, wetlands, and oceans. It encompasses mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, fish, insects, and various other organisms. Wildlife plays a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance, contributing to biodiversity, and providing essential ecosystem services.
